How it works

1

Download and open RemoteX.

2

It shows your Session ID and a one-time password.

3

Share both with your SL Brothers technician.

4

They connect with your permission. Disconnect anytime.

Before you start

🍎 Mac users — one extra step macOS may say "RemoteX is damaged". It isn't — the app just isn't Apple-signed yet. Drag RemoteX to Applications, then run this once in Terminal: xattr -cr /Applications/RemoteX.app and open it normally.
🪟 Windows users You may see "Windows protected your PC". Click More info → Run anyway. This appears because the app isn't code-signed yet — it isn't a virus.
🔒 Your privacy Connections are end-to-end encrypted. Your IP address is never shared with the technician. The microphone and speaker are off by default. Only accept a session from someone you know and trust.
